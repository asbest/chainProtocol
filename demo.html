Chain Protocol Demo - Multiplayer Chat

This demo illustrates how the decentralized blockchain protocol can be used for serverless multiplayer communication, such as a chat application. Each client maintains its own copy of the blockchain. Messages are added as blocks, and clients can sync their chains by exchanging the compressed blockchain string.
